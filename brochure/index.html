Forge Public Safety exists to give fire, EMS, and emergency management agencies one platform that actually matches how they work.

Forge was developed from real-world public safety experience — not from a generic software playbook. We understand shift schedules, compliance pressure, apparatus readiness, prevention workloads, and the reporting burden that pulls crews away from the mission.

Our platform connects personnel, training, operations, prevention, fleet, analytics, and command into one agency-ready system. Agencies start with a strong base package and add modules as their mission grows.
